About Luna & Bloom

Founded by floral artist Vyona Vee, Luna & Bloom began in a tiny sunlit studio with a dream: to make every bouquet feel like a personal love letter. We source seasonal, sustainable blooms and wrap them in silk ribbons and soft tissue.

Every arrangement is made with intention, joy, and a sprinkle of fairy dust 🌼
